Our Story

KAPE SINA UNA is a unique coffee shop originally situated on Marcos Highway in Antipolo City, where it offers a mesmerizing view of the surrounding mountains. Instead of a traditional storefront, it operates from the trunk of a parked MPV (multi-purpose vehicle), which has been cleverly converted into a coffee-serving hub. The setup includes an open trunk, an extended makeshift roof, and a handful of wooden tables and chairs. This unconventional setting distinguishes it from the usual family tailgate picnics you might encounter.

Accountant Mark Macul and engineer Mc-Neil Manauis are the men behind Kape Sina Una. According to him, they are still looking for a permanent place for their small business. They picked the current spot for its view. Some of their customers include bikers and cyclists taking a break from the physically demanding mountain roads of Antipolo.

They named their coffee shop Kape Sina Una after the Tagalog word “sinauna” which means “ancient”. Mark and Mc-Neil wanted a name that would reflect the surroundings, the “probinsiya feels” to be exact.

Our Vision

Our vision at Kape Sina Una is to redefine the coffee shop experience by offering a unique and memorable environment that blends nature, community, and great coffee. We envision our coffee shop as a hub for fostering connections, inspiring creativity, and promoting a deep appreciation for the art and science of coffee. We aim to become a trusted and respected name in the local coffee scene, known for our commitment to excellence, innovation, and sustainability. Through our passion for coffee and our dedication to customer satisfaction, we aspire to create a lasting impact on the coffee culture in Antipolo City and inspire a love for exceptional coffee among our patrons.

Our Mission

Our mission at Kape Sina Una is to create a warm and inviting space where coffee enthusiasts can gather, connect, and savor exceptional coffee experiences. We are committed to serving the finest locally sourced coffee, supporting local farmers, and promoting sustainable practices in the coffee industry. With a dedication to quality, creativity, and customer satisfaction, we strive to become a beloved destination for coffee lovers in Antipolo City and beyond.

Our Coffee

At Kape Sina Una, we take great pride in our coffee offerings. We believe that coffee is an art form, and we meticulously select and roast the finest locally sourced beans to bring out their unique flavors and aromas.

Our Partners

Our partnerships are integral to the success of Kape Sina Una, as they enable us to offer exceptional coffee, support local communities, and contribute to a sustainable coffee industry. We value these relationships and continually seek out new collaborations that align with our mission and vision.
